FAQs

Find answers to your questions about HACK HUSTLE!


Q: What is the event format?

      A: It’s time to step out! This is an offline event, so get ready to meet, greet, and hustle in person.

Q: Is there a registration fee?

      A: Yep, it’s a cool ₹400—basically the cost of a few pizzas, but much more exciting!

Q: Will accommodation be provided?

      A: On-campus snacks and food to keep you going, but no stay options—so find a cozy spot nearby!

Q: What facilities will be provided?

      A: Oh, you’re in for a treat! Expect awesome T-shirts and other swag to show off your hustle spirit!

Q: Can beginners participate in the event?

      A: Absolutely! Whether you’re a coding wizard or a curious newbie, we want you here. Join the fun!

Q: What types of projects are allowed?

      A: Software? Hardware? Heck, bring both if you want! We’re all about creativity.

Q: What essentials should I bring?

      A: Just pack your laptop and whatever else you think you’ll need for a full day of hacking magic!

Q: Will there be Wi-Fi available during the event?

      A: Yes, Wi-Fi will be your loyal companion throughout the event—stream, code, and conquer.

Q: Can I participate solo without a team?

      A: Sorry, lone wolves! You need at least 2 members to make up a team. Teamwork makes the dream work!(Max members = 4)

Q: Is travel cost sponsored?

      A: Sadly, no travel sponsorship—but the event will be worth every mile!

Q: Will there be mentorship sessions?

      A: Oh yes! We’ll have mentors to guide you like Gandalf on your hacking journey!

Q: Who can participate, students or professionals?

      A: It’s students only—so if you’re in the game, we’re ready to play!
